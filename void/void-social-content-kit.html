Drop Week Rule

Go heavy during drop week — 2 posts/day. Then go silent. The quiet after the drop is part of the brand language.

Non-Drop Week Rule

Max 3 posts per week. Never more. Scarcity applies to content too. Every post must earn its place.

The Silence Rule

At least one full silent day after every drop. No stories. No reposts. Complete absence. Let the audience feel it.
