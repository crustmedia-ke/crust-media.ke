About Yau
& You

Yau & You is a Southern-grown botanical tea brand crafted from native yaupon holly — delivering smooth, sustainable energy designed for modern living.

An alternative to overstimulation. A return to rhythm. Where other energy brands shout, Yau & You whispers. Where others spike, it flows. Rooted in the land, refined for the moment.

Core Pillars

What we
stand for

01

Botanical Roots

Yaupon holly is America's only native caffeinated plant. Yau & You honors that heritage — indigenous, resilient, and deeply tied to Southern soil.

02

Smooth Energy

No spike. No crash. The natural theobromine in yaupon delivers calm, sustained focus — energy that fits your rhythm rather than forcing a new one.

03

Sustainable Future

Yaupon is native, resilient, and responsibly harvested. We prioritize mindful sourcing and intentional production practices. Progress should never come at the expense of the land.

04

Clean Aesthetic

Our design reflects our philosophy: simple, balanced, and purposeful. From packaging to digital presence, we value space, clarity, and restraint. What we leave out matters as much as what we include.
